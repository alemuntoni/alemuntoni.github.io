◆ resize()

template<typename T >
void vcl::mesh::CustomComponentsVectorMap< T, true >::resize ( uint  size)

When the method resize() is called on the container of the T element, it is necessary to resize also all the vectors for each custom component of the T element.

At this call, we don't know statically the types of each custo component, therefore we cannot initialize them (std::any of each vector will be in an invalid state). Therefore, we mark all the resized custom components as 'needToInitialize'. Initiaization will be made just on the uninitialized values of the vectors at the first access of each custom component.
